# Code of Conduct

## Our Standard

This project welcomes contributors who want safer, more useful AI-assisted WordPress workflows.

Expected behavior:

- Be respectful and specific.
- Critique workflows, code, and documentation without personal attacks.
- Keep user safety, privacy, and maintainability first.
- Avoid sharing credentials, customer data, or private site materials.
- Be clear when reporting a risk, limitation, or failed verification.

Unacceptable behavior:

- Harassment, hate speech, threats, or personal abuse.
- Publishing secrets, private customer data, or exploit details.
- Encouraging unsafe live-site changes, credential leakage, or deceptive claims.
- Repeatedly ignoring maintainer guidance on scope, safety, or documentation quality.

## Enforcement

Maintainers may edit, close, hide, or remove issues, pull requests, comments, or releases that violate this code. Serious or repeated violations may result in a ban from the project.

## Reporting

For conduct issues, contact the maintainer privately where possible. For security vulnerabilities, follow `SECURITY.md`.
